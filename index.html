Smoking:

In this era of prompt development huaman society are the most interconnected, dynamic and proporous than it had ever been. However the problem that afflicts human society through its history has never stop. According to research 56 million people died in 2017 and a major cause to these were disease, for instance the Cardiovascular disease killed 17.79 million people and the respiratory disease killed over 6 million people (1). Two of them added could take over half of the death toll. What are the possible cause of these deadly diseases? It was shown that a major cause led to heart and lung disease was smoking. Smoking could led to cancer, heart disease, stroke lung disease, and chronic obstructive plumnory disease. A cessation of smoking is therefore a rational way to prevent these diseases. When we look into the realities of smoking it shows that nearly 9 out of 10 adult smoker started before age 18, and about 3 out of 4 high school smoker will become adult smoker (2). To reduce the prevelance of smoking, youths is where we should start.


How has different countries performed about smoking? Statistic shows that the share of smoker of men and women age 15 and older are around 25% in countries like China and Pakistan and around 35% in countries like France, German, and other European states. These showed a clear distinction between the two regions. Another set of data shows the sum of total applicable taxes on the weight of average price of tobacco.In countries like France, German and other European states taxation are at range of 70% to 90% while in countries like China and Pakistan the taxation are in range of 40% to 70% . It was clear that countries with greater percentage of smoker had impose heavier taxation. But despite of the high taxation why is the percentage of smoker still so high in European countries? The answer could probably be found from the per capita income of that region. Higher the per capita income could means greater flexiblity in consumption.In Europe where the per capita income is top in the world the percentage and prevalance of smoking is high.To further prove this we can take example of Africa. In Africa the prevalence of smoking is only around 5% to 10% and per capita income is few of the lowest in the world.This drawed a clear contrast between the two region



Other than the per capita income the voice and attitude of country's civil is a unignorable factor.The newspaper for example is a very important way in spreading awareness and create unity against smoking and other health issues.In Pakistan 30.4% of news coverage are on politics, 20.6% on governance, 14.9% on state institutions.8.3% on foreign policy, 6.65% on terrorism, 2.5% on corruption, 4% on non state institutions, 6% on violence and 4% on crime and injustice (4). The coverage for health and disease barely exist.This may led to a lack of puclic awareness of health and effects of smoking and consequently greater possibilty of smoking. In Pakistan 1,200 children are getting addicted to smoking,hope more awareness and preventive measures could be taken.


Pycologists shows that when a person smokes a dose of nicotine reaches the brain and would improve mood and concentration,decrease stress, and relax muscles for that moment.But in long term smoking may cause physical symptom like headache or breathlessness as well as making people feel irritable anxious or low. Could imagine what would happen to a student if they smokes at their exam season (5) >.



While Econmoists stated that without the cultivation of tobacco, manufacture of tobacco products and distribution and sale of products, a country's economy will suffer devastating economic consequences. Job will be lost, income will fall, tax revenue will plumnet (6).For a country's government it is a extremely difficult to choose between the publiuc health and country's economy, most countries prefer to keep a state of balance between the two which is what we see now a days.

About SSNGO


StopSmokingNGO! is a Global Perspectives Initiative started by three students.
Our aim is to spread awareness about the cultural perspectives as well as general information about smoking among teenagers.
All of our effort is to gauge statistics and possibly reduce the number of teenage smokers.
